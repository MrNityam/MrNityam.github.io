These Chrome Tricks will Blow your Mind

Open a New tab to test all the tricks by your own...

Trick 1 : To know about Chrome Version and about Chrome search chrome://version/



Trick 2 : To see all the extensions installed in your browser search chrome://extensions/



Trick 3 : To check the updates of components of chrome search chrome://components/



Trick 4 : To check the lists all modules loaded into the browser and renderer processes and modules registered to load at a later point. search chrome://conflicts/
